## PINTEREST AFFILIATE MONEY — the honest answer (research 2026-07-01)

# as PRIMARY income. Pinterest money = DISPLAY ADS, not affiliate.

HARD EVIDENCE (real itemized income reports, ads vs affiliate share):
- Midwest Foodie: $519k ADS / $8.9k affiliate = 97% ads.
- Rich And Delish 97.7% ads. Partylicious 97.7% ads. Fig Jar 93% ads (6.5%
  affiliate = the CEILING seen in food).
- Minimize My Mess (decor): affiliate "mostly failed/no conversions", $100-200/mo,
  ~15-20x more from ads.
- NEVER found a verified report where Pinterest was the PRIMARY driver of
  AFFILIATE (not ad) revenue for a normal niche. 6 yrs of Reddit threads = none.
- The only BIG "Pinterest affiliate" earners sell PINTEREST COURSES (Create and
  Go, The She Approach) = meta-income, not proof.

WHY: Amazon (the default Pinterest affiliate) = low rates (1-4.5%) AND bans
direct Pinterest links -> you must go pin->blog->link -> you build a blog anyway
-> ad revenue dominates. "Pinterest drives 79% of revenue" = AD revenue weighted
by Pinterest's high ad RPM, misread as affiliate.

THE REFRAME (model is right, engine is backwards):
- ADS = the engine (90-98% of income once at Mediavine/Raptive scale; Journey
  now opens at 1k sessions). AFFILIATE = garnish (~2-7%). DIGITAL PRODUCT = the
  breakout lever (RPM ~$535, 16x ads).
- So: build for AD revenue in a Pinterest-native, high-RPM niche; treat affiliate
  as a small top-up; add a cheap product.

WHERE AFFILIATE IS A REAL TOP-UP (click-REQUIRED content only):
- FOOD/recipe (kitchen gear, Amazon) — click required for ingredients.
- DIY/CRAFT tutorials (Cricut/craft supplies via ShareASale/Awin) — the BEST
  affiliate-side niche; once rivaled ads ($895 vs $1,055).
- Buying guides (home/garden/pet).
WHERE AFFILIATE FAILS: decor/fashion/quote INSPIRATION pins (self-contained,
~0.1% click-through vs ~5% recipes).

REINFORCES GAP-SCAN PICKS: SOURDOUGH (food = click-required + high ad-RPM +
product) and CROCHET (craft = best affiliate-side + product) are exactly the
niches where pins->blog->ads works AND affiliate tops up AND a product fits.
HEADWIND: Pinterest outbound clicks cratered for many bloggers since ~Feb 2025.

   Airtable, Shopify, Notion, niche industry-specific tools).

**Three income streams, staged:**
1. Affiliate signups — the initial money. (active focus)
2. Display ads on the blogs — turned on once traffic is real.
3. Brand deals — once the property has audience/authority.
