## 1. THESIS (decoupled)
The one place generic rave content makes PORTABLE money without the event moat =
the GEAR / buying-guide engine. Ranks on GOOGLE (buyer-intent, evergreen), converts
affiliate at 15-25% on gear, funded by a small anchor cluster. The empty seat the
research found — the "Wirecutter of rave gear," honest cross-brand reviews the
retailer blogs (iHeartRaves/iEDM/FRW) CAN'T do because it cannibalizes their apparel.
This asset lives or dies on its own content; the events are irrelevant to it.

## 2. THE ECONOMICS (honest — and why lever order FLIPS here)
Generic Pinterest-money finding = "ads are the engine (90-98%), affiliate is garnish."
That's TRUE for FOOD/craft (high ad RPM). It does NOT hold for rave: rave = bottom-
third ad RPM ($2-8 -> maybe $10-25 premium). So on a STANDALONE rave blog the levers
re-rank:
1. **AFFILIATE ON GEAR = the primary $** (15-25%, 30-45d cookie, buyer-intent reviews).
   This is the engine here, not the garnish — because ads are weak in this niche.
2. **DIGITAL PRODUCT = the breakout lever** (RPM ~$535, ~16x ads). A festival-prep
   system / packing planner / kandi pattern packs, $9-29. Doubles as email upsell.
3. **DISPLAY ADS = thin floor** once traffic scales (Journey@1k sessions -> Raptive@25k pv).
HONEST CEILING: $0-100/mo first 3-6mo; $100-600/mo for a validated small catalog yr 1;
real ad money is a year-2 event. ~80% quit ~month 18. Accept the curve or don't start.

## 4. THE 3-CHANNEL ENGINE (single-channel dependence kills the 95%)
- **PINTEREST = ignition (months 1-12).** Fresh pins = #1 lever. Live data: Pinterest
  discovery = OUTFITS + MAKEUP + KANDI only. Do NOT expect gear/reviews to get
  discovered on Pinterest — pin them, but they're Google terms. 3-5 fresh pins/day,
  bright/high-contrast 2:3, on the 3 reach posts.
- **GOOGLE SEO = compounding (yr 2+).** The gear reviews + hub. Buyer-intent, evergreen.
  This is where the standalone money actually compounds. AI for scaffolding ONLY —
  write the real experience/opinion yourself (HCU hit 71% of affiliate sites).
- **EMAIL = owned insurance (day 1).** Two lead magnets: kandi pattern PDF + festival-
  prep/harm-reduction checklist. List promotes the money content year-round, smooths
  the Feb-Oct seasonality.

## 8. STACK (LOCKED 2026-07-09)
- **Platform: MANAGED WORDPRESS on its OWN domain** — fully standalone, NOT on the
  flowstateslc.live Next app. Chosen over custom Next.js / builders because ads are
  treated as a REAL leg here: Mediavine/Journey/Raptive are WordPress-first (auto
  ad-placement + support), plus fastest publishing + the Lasso/Rank Math plugin
  ecosystem, with zero infra to manage. (Next.js was defensible since ads = floor and
  BLOG-SPEC's MDX system ports over — but managed WP won on ad-RPM + speed-to-volume.)
- **Host:** Cloudways (~$11-14/mo, DigitalOcean, managed + scales cleanly into Mediavine)
  — or SiteGround GrowBig for a cheaper/simpler managed start. Host handles updates/
  security/backups/caching.
- **Domain:** own domain (TBD — register at Cloudflare at-cost or Namecheap). Standalone
  brand, cleanly sellable as an asset later.
- **Theme:** GeneratePress or Kadence (lightweight, fast, ad-network-friendly).
- **Plugins:** Rank Math (SEO/schema, free) · Pretty Links (free) -> Lasso (paid, link
  mgmt + comparison tables, when revenue justifies) · image optimizer (ShortPixel/Imagify)
  · WP Rocket or host-level caching · MailerLite/Kit form plugin.
- **Email:** MailerLite -> Kit. **Pins:** Tailwind (~$18/mo, approved partner) or native.
  **Analytics:** GA4 + Search Console. **Ads ladder:** AdSense (Site Kit) -> Journey by
  Mediavine (~1k sessions) -> Mediavine (~50k sessions) / Raptive (~100k pv). Verify live
  thresholds before betting — they shift.
