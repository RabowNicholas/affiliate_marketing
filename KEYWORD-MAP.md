reviews (the 15-25% money) -> high-AOV camping (the committed).

  their JOB is reach + email capture, NOT direct conversion.

  commission; they need to be FED by the discovery content + email.

  linking to the PACKING LIST hub, which links out to the Tier-1 money reviews.

  behind opt-in; newsletter then promotes the money content. Cross-link to LED
  gloves (EmazingLights) + glitter (Today Glitter) which DO pay.

  gear reviews credible; each links contextually into a money page.
